About

Step into MythWalker, a groundbreaking geolocation fantasy RPG where every step in the real world brings you closer to unraveling Earth's deepest myths.

Guided by an ancient being, players traverse real-world locations to uncover secrets of Mytherra — a mysterious parallel realm. A cosmic threat now looms over both dimensions, and only you, the chosen Mythwalker, can restore balance. Form alliances, uncover ancient truths, and awaken heroes of legend in an immersive journey across worlds.

Features

  • Geolocation Adventure

    Move through real-world locations to trigger events and uncover hidden lore.

  • Augmented Reality Elements

    Encounter mystical gateways and entities layered onto your physical world.

  • Parallel World Exploration

    Traverse Mytherra, a fantasy version of Earth influenced by global mythologies.

  • Hero Summoning

    Recruit and bond with legendary figures from myths and folklore.

  • Battle Threats

    Face creatures born from myth in tactical real-time combat.

  • Team-Based Strategy

    Form squads of heroes with synergized abilities.

  • Lore-Rich Missions

    Engage in stories shaped by your journey and location.

  • Power Progression

    Level up, equip artifacts, and unlock hero memories.

How to play

  • Start your journey by activating GPS.

  • Explore your local area to trigger events and lore.

  • Encounter rifts and unlock Mytherra portals.

  • Recruit and upgrade legendary heroes.

  • Engage in battles and complete missions.

  • Discover hidden artifacts and mythical locations.

  • Save both Earth and Mytherra from looming catastrophe.
